Balkan-Style Beef Meatballs

Description

He shoots, you score! This winning recipe gets the nod from Nick Suzuki, Montreal Canadiens captain. Great balls of Balkans! Taking inspiration from southeastern European cultures, these ground beef marvels are brimming with deep-set flavours from two kinds of paprika (sweet and smoky), chili powder and minced garlic. Brown them on all sides before building a sauce straight in the same pan. Tomato sauce, demi-glace and more spices join chunks of sweet pepper, wilted greends and briny feta cheese. put this hearty party over wholesome bulgur and bliss out.

Ingredients

  • 250g ground beef
  • 120g baby greens
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 sweet pepper
  • 80g bulgur
  • 100ml tomato sauce
  • 30ml vegetable demi-glace
  • 60g feta
  • 9g smoky & sweet paprika spices
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the bulgur
    • In a medium pot, bring to a boil the bulgur, 1 1/2 cups of water, & a big pinch of salt
    • Reduce heat once boiling, cover & simmer for 6-8 minutes until bulgur is tender and water is absorbed
    • Fluff and keep warm
  2. Mise en place
    • Combine beef, 1/2 of the garlic, 2/3 of the spices, salt, & pepper
    • Form 16 meatballs
  3. Cook the meatballs
    • Heat oil in a large pan
    • Add meatballs, cook partially covered, turning occasionally, for 5-7 minutes
    • Cook until partially cooked
  4. Finish Meatballs & make sauce
    • Add sweet pepper & remaining garlic to pan of meatballs
    • Cook until fragrant, stirring frequently, for 30 sec to 1 minute
    • Add tomato sauce, demi-glace, 1/2 cup of water, & remaining spices
    • Cook, stirring occasionally, for 1-2 minutes
    • Add spinach & cheese
    • Cook, stirring occasionally, 2-3 minutes until meatballs are cooked through & spinach is wilted
  5. Plate your dish
    • Divide bulgur
    • Top with meatballs & sauce
