Souvlaki-Spiced Ground Pork Bowls

Description

This Greek-themed recipe is equally nifty and nutritious, and takes no time at all. In a neat move, bulgur gets top billing: cook the grain until fluffy, and then pump up the volume with grated succhini and feta. Check off another veg box with a salad of chopped tomatoes and cukes in a light vinaigerette. Peppery, herby, garlicky ground pork and a dollop of tzatziki will have everyone digging in for more.

Ingredients

  • 510g ground pork
  • 50g sliced red onions
  • 3 cucumbers
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 zucchini
  • 50ml cold-pressed italian vinaigrette
  • 160g bulgur
  • 120g garlic-cucumber yogurt (tzatziki)
  • 60g feta
  • 20g mediterranean shores spices
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the bulgur
    • Grate the zucchini
    • In a medium pot, bring to a boil the bulgur, 1 1/2 cups of water, & a big pinch of salt
    • Reduce heat once boiling, cover & simmer for 6-8 minutes until bulgur is tender and water is absorbed
    • Add zucchini and cheese to bulgur and fluff
  2. Cook the pork
    • In a large pan, heat oil
      • >
    • Add onions and saute until fragrant (30 seconds to 1 minute
    • Add pork, season with spices, salt, & pepper
    • Cook pork, breaking up the meat, 4-6 minutes
  3. Make the salad
    • Dice tomatoes and cucumbers
    • Combine cucumbers, tomatoes, & vinaigrette in a large bowl
  4. Plate your dish
    • Divide bulgur
    • Top with pork, salad, & tzatziki
